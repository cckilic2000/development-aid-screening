ADB Sovereign Project 57037-010 is a $1.25 million preparation activity for the Kyrgyz Republic's $39.5 million Skills Development System Transformation Program, planned for ADB Board approval in 2026. The activity covers program due diligence (including economic, financial, climate, and safeguards assessments), detailed engineering design and bidding documents for civil works, and institutional readiness support including institutional mapping, capacity assessment, and capacity development planning. Specific procurement packages and deadlines are detailed in the Procurement Plan document (Jun 2026) which is accessible via the ADB project page but not reproduced on the overview page.

## Detailed Assignment Summary

This preparation activity exists to ensure the timely and efficient launch of the larger $39.5 million Skills Development System Transformation Program by completing the due diligence and design work required for ADB Board approval in 2026. The activity is structured around three components.

Component (i) covers program due diligence: seismic engineering resources and assessments, disaster risk management analysis, climate change assessments, safeguards review, and economic and financial analysis. Component (ii) involves the detailed engineering design (DED) and preparation of bidding documents for civil works — these are consulting deliverables to prepare for eventual construction under the main program. Component (iii) provides institutional readiness support and program coordination, covering institutional mapping of the education system, capacity assessment of implementing institutions, stakeholder engagement planning, and the development of capacity development plans for the Ministry of Education and Science.

The Kyrgyz Republic is the beneficiary country and implementing context. The project page identifies a Project Officer (Omurzakova, Baktygul Muhamedovna, Sectors Department 3) as the ADB contact. Three documents are listed: the Procurement Plan (Jun 2026), the Activity Administration Manual (Dec 2025), and the Activity Report (Dec 2025).
