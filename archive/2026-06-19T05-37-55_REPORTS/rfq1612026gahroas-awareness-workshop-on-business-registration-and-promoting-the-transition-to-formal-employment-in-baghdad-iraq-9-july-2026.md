## Executive Summary

The ILO is seeking an organisation to deliver an awareness workshop on business registration and the promotion of formal employment in Baghdad, Iraq, scheduled for 9 July 2026. The procurement is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ/161/2026/GAH/ROAS) through the UNGM platform with a submission deadline of 29 June 2026. The assignment falls within ILO's mandate on decent work and enterprise formalisation in post-conflict MENA contexts. Full deliverable specifications are contained in tender annexes accessible through the ILO e-Sourcing Platform. Budget and contract value are not disclosed in the public notice.

## Detailed Assignment Summary

The assignment involves the organisation and delivery of a single awareness workshop targeting business registration processes and the transition of workers and enterprises into the formal economy in Baghdad. The event is scheduled for 9 July 2026. The ILO office in Iraq (GAH/ROAS) is the originating unit, and the contracting is handled by ILO Procurement centrally.

The UNGM notice does not reproduce the full Terms of Reference or scope specifications, which are attached to the tender in the ILO e-Sourcing (Jaggaer) platform. The geographic focus is Baghdad, Iraq. No information on target participant groups, number of participants, training content depth, or methodology is provided on the public notice page.
