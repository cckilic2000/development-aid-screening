## Executive Summary

WWF-Pakistan is seeking consultancy services for internal audit of the German Disposition Fund under the Billion Tree Afforestation Support Project. The assignment duration is six months, with up to six working days per month, and the RFP identifies a published amount of 225,000/-. The consultant is expected to verify transactions, assess compliance with KfW requirements and donor regulations, review internal controls and risk management, and produce monthly and final audit reports.

## Detailed Assignment Summary

The assignment objective is to strengthen accountability, transparency, and compliance in financial management by engaging an independent audit firm to undertake monthly internal audits of the German Disposition Fund managed under BTASP. The audit will verify transactions, assess compliance with KfW requirements, FMG, and applicable regulations, review the effectiveness of internal controls and risk management, identify gaps, recommend corrective actions, and provide independent assurance on financial management systems.

Specific tasks include developing annual and monthly internal audit plans; reviewing monthly financial reports, vouchers, bank reconciliations, and accounting records; verifying expenditure incurred by LSA#1 and LSA#2; reviewing fund flow, supporting documentation, and eligibility of expenditures; assessing compliance with FMG, KfW requirements, and donor regulations; reviewing previous audit observations and implementation status; and recommending improvements in governance, internal controls, and financial management.

## Key Deliverables

- Inception meeting and audit workplan.
- Monthly internal audit reports with findings and recommendations.
- Monthly expenditure verification certificates for LSA#1 and LSA#2.
- Compliance assessment reports.
- Risk and internal control improvement recommendations.
- Final consolidated audit report at completion of assignment.
